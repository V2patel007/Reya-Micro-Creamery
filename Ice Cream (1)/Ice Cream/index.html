About us 

“Reya, our daughter, our inspiration, is at the core of our ice creams. Her deep appreciation for life's simple pleasures and love for ice-creams has ignited our culinary creativity. We take great pride in introducing the Pacific Northwest to a blend of unconventional ice cream flavors influenced by South Asian, Indian, and Persian traditions. Each scoop represents a tribute to diverse cultures, enhanced with spices and unique combinations like Kesar Pista (saffron pistachio) or Paan (Betel Leaf). Committed to sustainability and supporting our local community, we create our vegetarian, no-egg delights using 100% locally sourced milk and cream right here in Washington State. We invite you to join us in this flavorful adventure and become a part of our story. Your thoughts and suggestions serve as the driving force that keeps our creativity flowing. — A joyous scoop of love from Reya and her family" .

Paan

Inspired by the traditional Indian mouth freshener and digestive aid, Paan ice cream is a novel and refreshing flavor. It is made by infusing the ice cream base with the flavors of betel leaves, gulkand (rose petal jam), areca nut (optional), and a hint of cardamom, creating a uniquely refreshing, sweet, and slightly spicy experience. The vibrant green color mirrors the freshness of betel leaves, and the inclusion of gulkand adds a floral sweetness, making it a perfect palate cleanser or a delightful end to a spicy meal. This flavor is a creative culinary interpretation of the paan's complex taste profile, offering an exotic, creamy dessert option.

Alphonso Mango

Celebrated as the king of mangoes, the Alphonso variety lends its rich, sweet flavor to this beloved ice cream. The flesh of ripe Alphonso mangoes is pureed and mixed into a creamy base to create a vibrant, naturally golden-hued dessert. This ice cream is all about the intense, aromatic sweetness and velvety texture of the mangoes, with each spoonful delivering a taste of tropical bliss. Often enjoyed during the mango season, Alphonso Mango ice cream is a refreshing and fruity treat that perfectly captures the essence of Indian summers.

Kesar Pista

A sublime fusion of fragrant saffron (kesar) and crunchy pistachios (pista), this ice cream flavor is a testament to the classic Indian palate. The creamy base is enriched with a generous amount of saffron strands, lending it a vibrant golden hue and a floral aroma. Chopped pistachios are sprinkled throughout, providing a nutty contrast to the smooth, creamy texture. Each bite is a blend of luxurious saffron's warmth and the earthy sweetness of pistachios, making Kesar Pista a timeless favorite.

Rajbhog

Inspired by a classic Bengali sweet, Rajbhog ice cream is a luxurious blend of creamy, saffron-infused ice cream, studded with chunks of soft cottage cheese dumplings. It's flavored with a mix of cardamom, pistachios, and saffron, offering a rich, aromatic experience. This flavor captures the essence of royal feasts, offering a taste of India's rich culinary heritage in every scoop.

Butterscotch

While not uniquely Indian, butterscotch ice cream has found a special place in India's dessert culture. This flavor is a rich, creamy concoction that features a sweet, buttery base interlaced with crunchy praline or butterscotch chips. The caramelized sugar and butter mixture lends it a deep, golden color and a decadent, smooth texture. Often drizzled with additional butterscotch sauce, this ice cream is a favorite for its perfect balance of sweetness and crunch, making it a beloved choice across generations.

Kaju Anjeer

This sophisticated ice cream flavor marries the rich, nutty taste of cashews (kaju) with the sweet, chewy texture of figs (anjeer). The base is a creamy, luxurious mixture where finely ground cashews are blended seamlessly, lending a subtle nuttiness. Chunks of dried figs are scattered throughout, offering bursts of sweetness and a slightly crunchy texture against the smooth ice cream. Kaju Anjeer is a celebration of texture and flavor, often enhanced with a hint of cardamom, making it a uniquely indulgent and aromatic dessert experience.
